Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar energy

How much does a solar system cost?

The cost of a solar system varies depending on size and type. A typical residential system costs between ₹1.5-5 lakhs. We offer financing options and can help you avail government subsidies up to 40%.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed within 1-3 days. Commercial systems may take 1-2 weeks depending on size and complexity.

What maintenance is required?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend cleaning the panels twice a year and an annual inspection to ensure optimal performance.

What happens during power outages?

Standard grid-tied systems shut down during outages for safety. If you need backup power, we can install a battery storage system or hybrid inverter.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes, all our solar panels come with 25-year performance warranty and 5-year manufacturing warranty. We also provide 2-year installation warranty.
